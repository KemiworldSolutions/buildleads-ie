FAQ

Where does the data come from?
The four Dublin local authorities publish a Weekly Planning List under statutory requirement. BuildLeads fetches those lists every Monday morning. Nothing on BuildLeads is private data.
How is this different from CIS or BCI?
Those services are excellent if you're a main contractor on a €5m project. They're overkill for a sole trader or small practice. BuildLeads does one city, one trade-relevant filter, one weekly email.
Can I see a sample?
Yes. Email hello@buildleads.ie with your trade and I'll send last week's full digest free.
What if I'm not in Dublin?
